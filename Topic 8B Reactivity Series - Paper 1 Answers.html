Question 3

💡 Explanation
Silver cannot displace copper. Copper cannot displace iron. Iron cannot displace aluminium. Magnesium is more reactive than zinc, so magnesium can displace zinc from zinc sulfate — this is the only correct statement.

Question 11

2024 P1 Q14
A metal reacts with another metal ion only if it is more reactive than that metal
Select the answer from your copy of the paper:
💡 Explanation
Z reacts with no solutions → Z is the least reactive. X and Y both displace Z²⁺ → both are more reactive than Z. X does not displace Y²⁺ → X is less reactive than Y. Increasing order of reactivity: Z → X → Y.
⚡ One-line Takeaway

In reactivity questions, rank metals by evidence: cold water > steam > acid > no acid reaction, and remember that ease of extraction is the reverse of reactivity.
