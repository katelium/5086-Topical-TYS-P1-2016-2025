Question 2

2017 P1 Q18
As alkane chain length increases: BP ↑, viscosity ↑, flammability ↓
Select the answer from your copy of the paper:
💡 Explanation
Larger alkane molecules have stronger intermolecular forces, so melting point and viscosity increase as n increases. They also become harder to ignite, so flammability decreases as the chain gets longer.

Methane reacts with chlorine in ultraviolet (UV) light. UV breaks Cl₂ into reactive chlorine radicals. It is a substitution reaction — a H atom in methane is replaced by a Cl atom.

Question 12

Homologous series: similar chemical properties, gradual change in physical properties
Select the answer from your copy of the paper:
💡 Explanation
Flammability generally decreases (not increases) as chain length increases. Melting point does not decrease with increasing chain length — it generally increases. Chemical properties are similar and physical properties change gradually. Viscosity increases as molecular mass increases.
